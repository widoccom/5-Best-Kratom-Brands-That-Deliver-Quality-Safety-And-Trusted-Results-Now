## Frequently Asked Questions

**What should I look for in kratom lab test results?**

Quality lab reports should include testing for heavy metals (lead, mercury, cadmium, arsenic), microbial contaminants (bacteria, yeast, mold), pesticide residues, and alkaloid content verification. Results should be recent (within 6-12 months) and from certified third-party laboratories rather than in-house testing.

**How do I know if a kratom brand is legitimate?**

Legitimate kratom brands display several key characteristics: comprehensive third-party lab testing, transparent business information including physical addresses, positive customer reviews across multiple platforms, responsive customer service, and compliance with American Kratom Association guidelines when applicable.

**What's the difference between kratom powder and capsules?**

Kratom powder offers more flexibility in dosing, faster absorption, and lower cost per gram, but requires measurement and has a bitter taste. Capsules provide convenient pre-measured doses without taste issues but cost more per gram and absorb more slowly due to the capsule shell.

**Can I trust online kratom vendors?**

Many reputable online kratom vendors operate with high standards of quality and safety. Look for vendors who provide detailed lab testing results, maintain professional websites with clear policies, offer responsive customer service, and have established positive reputations in the kratom community.

**How should kratom be stored to maintain quality?**

Store kratom in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and moisture. Use airtight containers to prevent exposure to air and humidity. Properly stored kratom can maintain potency for 1-3 years, while improper storage can lead to alkaloid degradation and potential contamination.

**What makes some kratom brands more expensive than others?**

Price differences typically reflect factors such as sourcing quality, testing comprehensiveness, processing methods, packaging quality, and business overhead. Premium brands often invest more in direct farmer relationships, extensive lab testing, small-batch processing, and quality control measures that justify higher prices.

**How can beginners choose the right kratom strain?**

Beginners should start with well-known strains like Red Bali for relaxation, Green Malay for balanced effects, or White Borneo for energy. Begin with small doses (1-2 grams), purchase from reputable vendors with good customer support, and consider sample packs to explore different varieties before committing to larger quantities.

Responsible kratom consumption involves understanding proper dosing, safety considerations, and best practices for optimal results.

**Dosage Guidelines**

Kratom dosing varies significantly based on individual factors including body weight, tolerance, and desired effects. General guidelines include:

**Beginner Doses**
- Start with 1-2 grams
- Wait 45-60 minutes before considering additional amounts
- Gradually increase by 0.5-1 gram increments
- Never exceed 5 grams in initial sessions

**Experienced User Ranges**
- Typical doses range from 2-8 grams
- Higher doses do not always produce better effects
- Individual tolerance varies significantly
- Regular users should monitor tolerance development

**Hydration Importance**

Kratom can have dehydrating effects, making adequate water intake essential:

- Drink 8-10 glasses of water daily when using kratom
- Increase fluid intake during hot weather or physical activity
- Monitor urine color as hydration indicator
- Consider electrolyte replacement during extended use

**Strain Rotation Benefits**

Rotating between different kratom strains helps prevent tolerance buildup:

- Use different strains on alternating days
- Avoid daily use of the same variety
- Take periodic breaks from kratom use
- Monitor effectiveness and adjust rotation schedule

**Avoiding Dangerous Combinations**

Kratom should not be combined with certain substances:

- **Alcohol** - Increased risk of respiratory depression
- **Prescription medications** - Potential for dangerous interactions
- **Other botanicals** - Unknown interaction effects
- **Stimulants** - Possible cardiovascular stress

Always consult healthcare providers before using kratom, especially when taking medications or managing health conditions.
